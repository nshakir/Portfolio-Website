CLIENT

Center of Collaborative Systems in Safety and Resiliance (CoSSar)

With group of 2 web designers and 2 web developers, we designed and built a responsive WordPress website for CoSSar. CoSSar is research lab facility that is apart of University of Washington and upon meeting the company, they only had a placeholder on the UW's website.

My role in this project was to collaborate with the other web designer in learning the goals of CoSSar by constructing user personas, building information architecture, designing wireframes and visual designs and working with the development team to building a prototype and eventually intergrating the prototype to a WordPress theme.

I was also the point of contact with Chris Hussein of CoSSar. Once our team completed a task, I would deliver and streamline any questions between Chris and the team to ensure that the project would run smoothly.
