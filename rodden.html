E-Commerce UX Case Study

Mendenall & Rodden

Mendenhall & Rodden involved creating a fictional women hiking apparel online retailer to study the elements of user experience, from developing business goals to the customer checkout process. The process included defining site objectives and understanding user needs, creating an effective information architecture, building wireframes, designing visual design, and hand-coding a click through protosite.

I named the mock website after two pioneer women hikers from the turn of the century

UX ANALYSIS

I had to build a website where I had to think of the users needs from searching for items and through the purchasing process. I had to form an information architecture, inventory, and build a seemless website for the user to go through. I used Axure to build the wireframes/visual design and used HTML/CSS3 to build the prototype website.
