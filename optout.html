CLIENT

Seattle Opt Out Group
75% Completed

Seattle Opt Out is a new Seattle organization that wants the public to be aware of standardized testing in the education system. They believe the source of funding for standardized testing should be utilized in other ways to benefit the growth of today's youth.

For this website redesign, I researched more about Seattle Opt Out as an organization, and standardized testing in the Seattle area as well as across the country. User personas focused on educators, parents, and students in which helped me build an information architecture for the website. Seattle Opt Out wanted a website as simple as possible, and wanted a color palette that focused around the color red. I designed a color palette was the warm, inviting, and had a aqua and green theme that symbolized "Emerald City".

Seattle Opt Out has a big following with the Hispanic community, but didn't know of a way to show their content without hiring a translator to do it for them. I installed a Google Translate javascript plugin in order for each page to be able to translate to Spanish without having Seattle Opt Out hire someone to continually translate their content.

UX ANALYSIS

Seattle Opt Out wants to provide information to educators, students and parents about standardized testing in the Seattle area. Their goal is to provide events, and information about quarterly testing and sources about how to "opt out" from these tests.

USER PERSONAS

The Educator

The educator would be people that are aware of the testing in their specific school but is looking at the various other tests that are provided in different schools. They will seek information and see if they would like to participate in opting out in their school.

The Curious Parent

The Parent would be someone seeking more information about opting out their children from standardized testing. They will seek forms, and see the various testing and when they will be provided in that given year. For the parents who speak Spanish, they will see the Google Spanish Translator in order to read the information.
