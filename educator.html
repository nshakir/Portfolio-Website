CLIENT

I Am An Educator
65% Completed

Jesse Hagopian is an activist, public speaker, contributing author and educator in Seattle, WA. Jesse is an adviser to the Black Student Union, founding member of Social Equality Educators, active member of the Black Lives Matter movement and Seattle Opt Out movement

Jesse's goal for the website redesign was to be able to find the best way for the user to read his content, watch videos and learn about the movements and organizations he is apart of.

My role in this redesign was to find a new way to display Jesse's content, whether it be speeches, videos, radio spots, or published work. The key was for the user to be able to find what they were looking for in the easiest way. I performed a content audit of the website and built a navigation system. Jesse and I agreed to have the front page display information in the shortest way, thus building a wireframe with a sidebar. Jesse decided that he wanted purple and greys in the color palette, in which I designed a color palette of deep purple, white black and grey. I designed a clean, modern visual design and I am currently building the prototype before the WordPress integration.

UX ANALYSIS

Jesse has several publications, videos, and needed a way to display this information in a simple matter for the user to dive into the information they were seeking. Jesse wants to draw people to his website that wants to learn more about various human rights movements and organizations. He also recently written a book and wanted to have the user be able to access videos of his speeches, and written text.

USER PERSONAS

The Activist

Activists would be seeking information about what Jesse has to offer in terms of the different organizations and movements he stands for. They will seek publications and video and would be local as well as not.

The Educator

Their interest would be to find more information in Jesse's educational career and his participation in local education movements such as Seattle Opt Out.
