CLIENT

Motorless Bicycle Company
75% Completed

Motorless City Bicycle Company is full service bicycle shop in Detroit, MI. Their main focus for their website to give customers the capabilities to make reservations for bicycle repairs online, and to form a blog that will attract bicycle enthusiasts in the Detroit area.

My role was to do build a redesign from the ground up. With this company being in an unfamiliar area, I did research with other local bicycle shops in the Detroit area and interviewed bicycle enthuasiasts of different ages to construct user personas. With these user personas, I built an information architecture, a navigation system; in order for the different user personas can use the website as they see fit. The team at Motorless City Bicycle has an eye for color and with this, I designed 3 color visual designs using their given color palette. I am currently building a prototype and once approved I will intergrate the prototype into WordPress.

This was my first remote freelance job. I corresponed with Motorless City through emails which has taught me that this could be an avenue that I would like to try when obtaining future freelancing opportunities.

UX ANALYSIS

The client wanted to have the user go to the website for information and a reference guide to the bicycle culture around Detroit. One goal was to have their online bike repair reservation app draw more business into their brick or mortar. Another goal was to have the user find information in less than 3 clicks.

USER PERSONAS

The Involved Rider

The involved rider wants to experience Detroit via the bicycle community through events in the city such as Slow Roll. The involved rider are people that are new to Detroit, or live outside of Detroit. Will probably want to partake in events all over the city.

The Young Rider

The young rider wants to be able to use their bicycles as much as possible due to environmental, health or affordability reasons. Likes to learn about what’s new and in trend. The young rider will more than likely be local in Detroit.

The Neighborhood Rider

Wants to be able to use their bicycles in the most efficient and affordable way. This might be their main or second form of transportation and will probably be local. They are apart of the community and will usually be informed of local events.
