Nectar has always had an interest in building things, whether it be through websites, short films, film trailers, photography, woodshop or steel welding. Nectar was inspired to work on short films and went to school for Film Editing at Columbia College Chicago. This experience has shown Nectar that the most effective design can be the most simple and that one’s voice can be conveyed in the shortest, most simple way.

After moving to Seattle, WA, Nectar decided to retrace her footsteps of early interests in web design and decided to join the program at Seattle Central. From her experiences, Nectar uses her creative eye, and experience to edit to build a beautiful, simple but effective websites.
